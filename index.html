Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about shopping with TOUCHWOOD OUTFIT

How do I choose the right size?

We provide detailed size charts for each product. Measure your chest, waist, and length according to our guide. If you're between sizes, we recommend going one size up for a comfortable fit. Our customer support team is also available to help with sizing questions.

What are your delivery options and timeframes?

We offer fast delivery across India, typically within 3-7 business days. We provide both prepaid and Cash on Delivery (COD) options. COD is available for orders up to ₹5000. You'll receive tracking information once your order is shipped.

Is Cash on Delivery really available?

Yes! We offer Cash on Delivery across India for your convenience. Simply select COD at checkout and pay when your order arrives at your doorstep. COD charges may apply depending on your location and order value.

What is your return and exchange policy?

We offer easy returns and exchanges within 7 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and in original condition with tags attached. Return shipping is free for defective items. For size exchanges, we'll guide you through the simple process.

What makes your products premium quality?

We use high-quality fabrics like premium cotton blends, ensure proper stitching and finishing, and conduct quality checks before shipping. Our products are designed to be durable, comfortable, and maintain their shape and color after multiple washes.

Do you offer customer support?

Absolutely! Our customer support team is available via WhatsApp, email, and phone to help with sizing, orders, returns, or any questions. We're committed to providing you with an excellent shopping experience from start to finish.
